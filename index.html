Problem & Opportunity

The demand for autonomous cars is growing and the existence of smart networks, such as the Aveiro Tech City Living Lab (ATCLL) network, creates a possibility for connection and mutual optimization of both technologies.

Due to failures on the part of vehicle sensors and/or failure to map the various cities, numerous errors are found that lead to previous projects failing.

Therefore, our group decided to take the opportunity to develop and possibly solve the lack of efficiency of these same sensors, making vehicle automation viable and safe.

Tasks

Define the architecture, the interaction between components and how they interact with each other.

Use and improve detection systems for people, vehicles and obstacles, based on deep learning algorithms.

Task 5

Task 6

Perform tests in simulation environments, laboratory and infrastructure of the city of Aveiro.
